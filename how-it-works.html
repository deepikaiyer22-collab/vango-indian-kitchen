How It Works

Ridiculously simple, on purpose

You make three choices. We handle everything else.

01

Choose a date

Pick an evening or a weekend slot that works for you and your group.

02

Choose a cuisine

North Indian or South Indian — whichever story you're curious about.

03

Choose your group size

Just you, a couple of friends, or the whole office. All welcome.

That's it. We'll handle the rest.

Once you reserve, the exact location gets shared with you by email — think of it as a small surprise. All the ingredients, spices, aprons, and equipment are ready before you walk in. You don't need to know how to cook. You just need to be curious and a little hungry.

By the end of the evening, you'll have cooked a full 3-4 course meal with your own hands, eaten it around one table with people you didn't know a few hours earlier, and picked up a recipe you can actually make again at home.
